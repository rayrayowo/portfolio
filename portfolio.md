## Projects

### 1. Trade Wizards - Stock Visualization & Strategy System
**GitHub**: [rayrayowo/stock-visualization](https://github.com/rayrayowo/stock-visualization)

A node-based trading strategy sandbox with neural network decision nodes for stock signal generation.

**Tech Stack**: Python, Pandas, NumPy, scikit-learn, Matplotlib, Power BI

**Key Features**:
- **Node-Based Architecture**: Compose trading strategies using indicator, compare, logic, math, and neural_net nodes
- **Neural Network Decision Node**: Rolling walk-forward MLP that predicts stock movement probability
- **Backtesting Engine**: Portfolio metrics including cumulative return, max drawdown, daily/trade win rate, and holding days
- **Data Pipeline**: Automated cleaning, feature engineering, and caching for reproducible runs
- **Visualization**: Entry/exit markers on price charts, correlation heatmaps, regime analysis

**Results**:
- Backtested on 2013-2018 S&P 500 data (~619,000 rows)
- Achieved 22.5% cumulative return with 57.7% trade win rate
- Max drawdown: 7%
